Workshop on Image Processing using MATLAB

The Communications Society Chapter under IEEE - VBIT SB, is organizing a two-day workshop on Image Processing using MATLAB. This workshop acquaints participants with various techniques such as feature extraction, edge detection and image visualization. It offers an invaluable opportunity for the students to improve their technical acumen by analyzing and solving complex problems.

Date: 28th February, 2024 and 29th February, 2024.
Timings: 10:00 AM to 4:20 PM.
Venue: Nalanda Auditorium, Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology.

Note:
1. There is no registration fee.
2. Register as a team of two.
3. One laptop per team is mandatory.
